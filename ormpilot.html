FAQ

Common questions
answered.

How long does setup take? +
Setup takes about 15 minutes. You'll connect your Google Business Profile, Yelp, and/or Facebook accounts via OAuth — no technical knowledge required. The moment you connect a platform, ORMPilot starts pulling in your existing reviews and sets up monitoring for new ones.
Does the AI always sound like my business? +
Yes. During onboarding you set your business's tone — Professional, Friendly, Formal, or you write a custom instruction (e.g. "We always call customers by first name and sign off with 'The [Business] Team'"). The AI uses your business name, your tone, and the reviewer's name and feedback to generate a response that sounds like you wrote it.
Can I respond directly from ORMPilot — or do I have to log into each platform? +
For Google and Facebook, you can publish responses directly from ORMPilot with one click — no need to log into those platforms. For Yelp, we generate a deep link that takes you directly to the Yelp response page with your draft pre-filled (Yelp's API restricts direct publishing for third-party tools).
Is ORMPilot HIPAA-compliant for dental / medical practices? +
ORMPilot includes a HIPAA-aware mode for dental and medical accounts. When enabled, AI responses never reference specific treatments, diagnoses, or any patient health information. We also provide advisory prompts during setup. We strongly recommend having your legal team review any workflow involving patient communications. We do offer BAAs for qualifying accounts — contact us for details.
Will my customers know the response was AI-generated? +
No. ORMPilot drafts a response — you review and publish it under your business name. Every response you send goes through you before it goes live. The AI is a drafting assistant, not an autonomous poster. Customers see a response from your business, not from any AI tool.
How does the review request campaign work? +
You upload a CSV of customer contacts (name + phone/email) or connect your POS system. ORMPilot sends a personalized SMS or email with a direct link to your preferred review platform (Google by default). We enforce a 90-day suppression window so no customer receives more than one request per 90 days. You can also schedule drip campaigns to send at a controlled pace (e.g. 10/day).
What happens after the 14-day trial? +
At the end of your trial you'll be asked to choose a plan. If you don't select one, your account moves to a read-only mode — you can still see your reviews but won't be able to send responses or review requests. No charges happen automatically. You won't be billed without explicitly choosing a plan and entering your payment details.
Can I use ORMPilot for multiple business locations? +
Yes. The Growth plan supports multi-location businesses and includes separate dashboards per location. The Agency plan includes up to 15 locations plus a white-label client portal. If you manage more than 15 locations, contact us for custom enterprise pricing.
